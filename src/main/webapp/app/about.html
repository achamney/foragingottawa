Primarily, the goal of Foraging Ottawa is to bring folks together with a common interest. "Together" could mean discussions occurring in the forums, it could mean sharing forage sightings and locations, or it could mean everyone getting together to go mushroom hunting.

Ideally, every visitor of Foraging Ottawa would learn that:
  1. That there is an abundance of edible goods waiting to be harvested in Ottawa.
  2. There is an army of like-minded individuals in Ottawa who are equally excited about this abundance.
  3. We could all use a bit of help learning about the environment, and what it can provide for us.

About Us:

We are eccentric locals who love everything that Ottawa has to offer. As a GIS analyst, and a GIS developer respectively, we love anything spatial in nature. And what is more spatial than harvesting locations within Ottawa!
